A Non Profit Organization geared toward helping young startups build web and mobile apps for free. The birth of the internet has allowed businesses to move onto a virtual space. As more businesses are making a shift to online and digital products, we are here to make sure that you have the opportunity to thrive in this marketplace by offering to build these products for you for free. For eligible startups, ofcourse.
